WHAT WE OFFER

  • FLEXIBLE HOURS

    You know there’s life after work, right? You’ll get to create your own work-life balance with our flexible schedule. We help you by limiting working hours to 30h/week. That should give you plenty of time to eat, sleep, and be merry.

    Work from anywhere, anytime. You’re free to spend the whole day with your dog on your lap, in your pajamas or on a beach chair sipping a (Virgin) Mai Tai. Just don’t forget your laptop!

  • MENTORING

    We love what we do, and we love sharing our knowledge. Our lead engineers are well versed in the latest technologies and engineering methods and happy to give you all the support and direction you need.

  • INTERESTING
    PROJECTS

    You get to choose what you want to work on (and what not to work on). We focus on our software developers’ interests when we decide how to distribute our jobs and help them nurture their passions.

WHO WE ARE

We are an international software development company based in Estonia by the name of Hyperbach Services OÜ. We’re also branded as 1gency on Upwork.

  • We focus mostly on Clojure and Python-based web development projects. We serve clients from all over the world without exception. So far, we’ve worked with individuals and companies from the US, Norway, Kenya, Russia, Albania, to name only a few.
